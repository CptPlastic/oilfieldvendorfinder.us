Tubing Inspection Services

Tubing Testers EMI, Inc.

Tubing Testers E.M.I. units perform rig floor electromagnetic inspection while the tubing is being pulled out of the well. The inspection head is easily lifted onto the rig floor by the rig hoist line and positioned over the slips. E.M.I. scanning detects tubing wall loss caused from rod wear, pitting, and corrosion that can lead to future well failures. Our units utilize a compact inspection head, solid state wall monitoring, 3-deminsional flaw detection, split check, and real time data presentation at speeds up to 250 ft./min. We can inspect all tubing sizes from 1 ½” to 3 ½”. Upon the completion of a job, the customer will receive a custom “Well Profile” inspection report for continuous well management. Our E.M.I. units are continually upgraded with the latest technology in the industry.
