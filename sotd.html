Inspection Services

Contractors Quality Inspections, LLC.

Contractors Quality Inspections, LLC. (CQI) is an established Quality Assurance Inspections Company. CQI is classified as a woman owned Texas business, fully certified, licensed, and insured to do business worldwide. We provide Quality Assurance & Quality Control services to the Oil & Gas and Pipeline Segments of the Petroleum Industry. Our Quality Assurance inspectors and consultants have experience in project management, quality surveys, procedure writing, manufacturing, machining, NDT, training, and inspection processes used to qualify material to the individual customer specifications and or to the industry codes. CQI's other services include inventory, auditing, expediting, coordinating, consulting, production, safety and document control. When choosing CQI you will be receiving reliable, efficient, and comprehensive quality inspections that guarantee customer satisfaction, project success, and increased profitability.
