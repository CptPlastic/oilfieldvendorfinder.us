Hydrostatic Testing

Tubing Testers, Inc.

Tubing Testers Hydrostatic test units perform testing on oilfield tubing and casing while it is being pulled out or ran back into the well. Our hydrostatic test units are equipped with a custom “2 hose” bar tool system that is designed and manufactured in house. This allows our units to isolate the packer pressure from the tubing pressure to ensure the most accurate test in the industry. Our Units can test all sizes from 1 ½” to 4 ½” at pressures up to 15,000 p.s.i. In addition, we can test salta-lined, I.P.C., ceramic lined, poly core, and hydril tubing.
